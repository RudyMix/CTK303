All I Have

Jennifer Lopez ft. LL Cool J

A History

Released on December 14, 2002 and hitting #1 in early March of 2003, "All I Have" by Jennifer Lopez and LL Cool J exploded on the charts. It was originally made to be on J. Lo's next album but wound up as a single due to the record company convincing her to release it as such. It remained at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 for four weeks, was nominated for two Teen Choice Awards, and won a BDSCertified Spin Award, an ASCAP Pop Music Award, and a BMI Pop Award by 2004.
